## Smart Savings Wallet

Create a beautiful and interactive savings wallet using **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript** with smooth modern animations.

### Income Detection

Whenever a new income transaction is added to the user's account, display a button labeled:

**"Add to My Wallet"**

When the user clicks the button:

* Open a modern popup/modal.
* Allow the user to enter or select the amount they want to save.
* After confirmation, immediately move that amount into the Savings Wallet.
* Deduct the selected amount from the available balance and add it to the wallet balance.
* Update all balances instantly with smooth counting animations.

### Wallet Animation

The wallet should visually grow and become more valuable as the user's savings increase.

Level 1:

* Small brown leather wallet.
* Simple appearance.

Level 2:

* Wallet becomes larger.
* Better texture.
* Small gold details appear.

Level 3:

* Wallet becomes dark brown with shiny golden edges.
* Gold coins begin appearing around it.

Level 4:

* Wallet transforms into a luxurious golden wallet.
* Premium glow effect.
* Floating sparkles.
* Coin shine animation.
* Soft breathing animation.

The transition between levels should be smooth and animated instead of changing instantly.

### Coin Animation

Whenever money is added:

* Gold coins should fall into the wallet.
* Coins should bounce naturally.
* Small sparkle particles should appear.
* Display a floating success message such as:

**"SAR 200 has been added to your Savings Wallet."**

### Savings Goal

Allow the user to set a savings goal.

Display:

* Current savings.
* Goal amount.
* Percentage completed.
* Animated progress bar.

When the goal reaches 100%:

* Play a premium celebration animation.
* Wallet glows brightly.
* Golden particles appear.
* Display:

**"Congratulations! You reached your savings goal."**

### Monthly Report

At the end of every month automatically generate a savings summary showing:

* Total income.
* Total amount saved.
* Saving percentage.
* Number of saving actions.
* Remaining balance.
* Progress toward the goal.

Display this information inside a beautiful dashboard card.

### Smart Insights

Generate intelligent suggestions based on saving behavior, for example:

* "You're saving more than last month."
* "If you continue this habit, you'll reach your goal in 2 months."
* "Great job! Your savings rate increased by 15%."
