2. Engineer features from consecutive months per user:
   - spend_ratio (current month)
   - savings_ratio (current month)
   - ent_change = pct_change in entertainment vs prev month
   - food_change = pct_change in food vs prev month
   - spend_change = diff in spend_ratio vs prev month
   - savings_change = pct_change in savings vs prev month
   - spend_volatility_3m = rolling 3m std of total_spending/income
   - spend_slope_3m = np.polyfit slope of spend_ratio over last 3 months
3. Target: next month remaining_balance < 10% of income
4. Train GradientBoostingClassifier(n_estimators=200, max_depth=3,
   learning_rate=0.05, random_state=42)
5. Decision threshold: 0.33 (optimized for recall)
6. If there are fewer than 20 (user, month) training rows after feature engineering
   (e.g. right after a fresh seed), skip training and fall back to a simple heuristic
   probability instead of crashing on an empty/tiny training set.
7. Store model + scaler in memory as module-level singletons
